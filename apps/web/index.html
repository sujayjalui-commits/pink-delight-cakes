About the baker

Baked from home, designed with care, and made for real celebrations.

Pink Delight Cakes began as a small home startup for family birthdays and special weekends. What stayed the same as orders grew was the attention to flavor, finish, and making each customer feel heard.

Pink Delight Cakes grew from family celebrations into a warm home bakery focused on soft finishes, homemade flavor, and cakes that feel truly personal.

Founder note

Pinky Sangoi handles each inquiry like a real conversation, not a rushed checkout. The aim is simple: make the cake feel personal, taste homemade, and arrive as part of the celebration instead of just another order.

  • Fresh sponge baked in small batches
  • Designs tailored to your event and color palette
  • Friendly ordering through direct message
